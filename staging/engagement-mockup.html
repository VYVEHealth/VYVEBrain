How your score works

Your score runs from 50 to 100. Everyone starts at 50 — even after a quiet week, the floor holds. The top 50 points come from your activity over the last 7 days.

Base points come from logging activity across six pillars: Today's Focus (5pts each), Daily Habits (1pt each), Body, Mind, and Connect (2pts each), plus your weekly and monthly check-ins. Daily caps apply so a single huge day doesn't dominate.

Two multipliers shape your final score. Consistency (×0.85 to ×1.30) rewards spreading activity across days. Variety (×0.90 to ×1.20) rewards using more than one pillar.

You can't game it. If you don't earn base points, the multipliers have nothing to multiply. If you only use one pillar, you cap below the strongest scores. The members at 95+ are the ones genuinely using all of VYVE.
